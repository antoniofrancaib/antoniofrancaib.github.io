    BSc student at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam | Striving to exploit the plasticity of the human brain

    Hello! I'm Antonio, a mathematician with a deep passion for understanding the nature of intelligence. I am intrigued by the exploration of the computational principles of natural and artificial intelligence, aiming to "reverse engineer" the brain's algorithms, both to learn about how our brains work and to build more effective AI systems. My greatest career goal is to leverage AI to help people gain greater agency over their circumstances and reach their full potential.

    To recharge my batteries, I spend time outdoors running, working out, surfing, traveling, reading and hanging out with friends.

I am Javier Rando Ramírez (aka Javier Rando or Javi Rando). I am a Computer Scientist with main focus in Artificial Intelligence. After obtaining a 
BSc in Data Science at Pompeu Fabra University (Barcelona, Spain), I am currently pursuing a MSc in Computer Science at 
ETH Zürich (Switzerland).

My goal is to shape a safe future for Artificial Intelligence ([why this is relevant](https://waitbutwhy.com/2015/01/artificial-intelligence-revolution-1.html)). I have wide experience with 
state-of-the-art language models, Transformers and Adversarial Attacks.

I am also Co-Founder at [EXPAI](https://expai.io/), a company offering Explainable AI services to companies that 
are willing to implement trustworthy AI solutions to increase efficiency and user trust.

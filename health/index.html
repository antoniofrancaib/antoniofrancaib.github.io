Workout Planning

I structure workouts heuristically: 3-4 strength sessions weekly (e.g., compound lifts like squats, deadlifts) alternating with 2-3 cardio days (HIIT or steady-state running). Volume progresses based on recovery metrics from wearables, aiming for 10-15% weekly increases. This aligns with recent meta-analyses on progressive overload for metabolic health.

Daily Habits

  • Sleep: 7-9 hours, tracked via Oura ring; blue-light block post-8 PM.
  • Hydration: 2-3L water, infused with electrolytes.
  • Mindfulness: 10-min meditation; intermittent fasting (16:8 window) for metabolic flexibility.
  • Movement: 10k steps daily, plus mobility work.

These habits stem from longitudinal studies showing cumulative benefits for hormonal balance and longevity.

Nutrition Protocols

I incorporate evidence-backed foods for anti-inflammation, metabolism, and hormonal support, based on 2023-2025 research. Portions: 1-2 servings daily per food.

  • Nori Seaweed:
    Rich in polysaccharides; supports gut health and vitamin B12 in vegetarians. Daily: 5g in salads or wraps to ameliorate colitis via microbiota modulation.
  • Turmeric:
    Curcumin drives anti-aging and metabolic benefits; I consume 1g with piperine. In teas or curries for longevity effects via oxidative stress reduction.
  • Lion's Mane Mushroom:
    Neuroprotective; enhances cognition and mood. 1g extract in coffee for acute mental energy.
  • Matcha:
    Catechins boost mood and anti-inflammatory activity. 2g daily in lattes for stress reduction and neuroprotective effects.
  • Pure Cacao:
    Flavanols support bone health; 20g dark (85%+) for fracture risk reduction. In smoothies for cardiovascular benefits.
  • Goji Berry:
    Polyphenols promote anti-photoaging and neuroprotection. 20g in yogurt for Parkinson's-like symptom mitigation via lipid peroxidation inhibition.

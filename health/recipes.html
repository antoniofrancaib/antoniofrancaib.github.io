Electrolyte-Rich Smoothies

Perfect for starting your morning hydrated and energized. These smoothies are packed with natural electrolytes to fuel your day.

Green Coconut & Banana Smoothie

This smoothie is packed with potassium and magnesium from the banana, coconut water, and spinach, making it an excellent electrolyte replenisher.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut water (unsweetened)
  • 1 large handful of fresh baby spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup frozen mango or pineapple
  • 1 scoop of vanilla or unflavored protein powder (optional)
165 Calories
2g Protein
40g Carbs
0.5g Fat

Watermelon & Berry Recovery Smoothie

Watermelon is incredibly hydrating and a natural source of electrolytes. Combined with berries and yogurt (which contains calcium), this makes for a refreshing morning drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut water (unsweetened)
  • 1 cup seedless watermelon chunks, frozen
  • ½ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries), frozen
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • A small pinch of sea salt (optional, for extra sodium)
185 Calories
10g Protein
32g Carbs
2g Fat
